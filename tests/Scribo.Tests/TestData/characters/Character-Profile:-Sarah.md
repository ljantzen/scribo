# Character Profile: Sarah

## Description

Sarah is a determined and curious protagonist in her early thirties. She has a sharp mind and an insatiable thirst for uncovering hidden truths. Her appearance is unassumingâ€”average height, brown hair that she often ties back, and intelligent eyes that miss nothing.

## Background

Sarah grew up in a small town, always feeling like there was more to her family's history than what she was told. After her grandmother's passing, she discovered a mysterious letter that set her on a path to uncover long-buried family secrets. She works as a research librarian, which gives her access to historical archives and documents that prove invaluable in her quest.

## Personality Traits

- **Curious**: Always asking questions and digging deeper
- **Persistent**: Never gives up when she believes something is important
- **Analytical**: Excellent at connecting seemingly unrelated pieces of information
- **Empathetic**: Cares deeply about the people affected by the secrets she uncovers
- **Cautious**: Understands that some truths can be dangerous

## Motivations

Sarah's primary motivation is to understand her family's true history and to ensure that the truth, no matter how painful, is brought to light. She believes that secrets have a way of causing harm, and that understanding the past is essential for moving forward.

## Relationships

- **Grandmother (deceased)**: Left behind clues that started Sarah's journey
- **Mother**: Keeps many secrets, creating tension between them
- **Research Colleague**: Provides support and access to resources
- **Mysterious Contact**: Someone who seems to know more than they're telling

## Character Arc

Sarah begins as someone who trusts authority and family narratives, but as she uncovers more secrets, she learns to question everything and trust her own instincts. Her journey transforms her from a passive observer to an active seeker of truth.
